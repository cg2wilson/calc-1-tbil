Theorem 2.4.2. Derivative Rules, Part 2.

The following are “base” functions and their derivatives.
Function Derivative
\(\sin (x)\) \(\cos (x)\)
\(\cos (x)\) \(-\sin (x)\)
\(\tan (x)\) \(\sec^2(x)\)
\(\cot(x)\) \(-\csc^2(x)\)
\(\sec(x)\) \(\sec(x)\tan(x)\)
\(\csc(x)\) \(-\csc(x)\cot(x)\)
\(e^x\) \(e^x\)
\(a^x\) \(\ln(a)\cdot a^x\)
\(\ln(x)\) \(\dfrac{1}{x}\)
\(\log_b(x)\) \(\dfrac{1}{\ln(b)}\cdot \dfrac{1}{x}\)
