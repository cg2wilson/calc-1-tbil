Definition 3.5.1. Indeterminate Form (0/0 and \(\infty/\infty\)).

An indeterminate form of the type 0/0 is a limit of the form
\begin{equation*} \ds \lim_{x\to a} \dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)} \end{equation*}
where \(f(x)\to 0\) and \(g(x)\to 0\text{,}\) or \(f(x)\to \pm \infty\) and \(g(x)\to \pm \infty\)
