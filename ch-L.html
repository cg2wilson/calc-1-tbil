    Chapter 1 Limits (L)

    Objectives

    This module describes the concept of and use of a fundamental concept in Calculus- the idea of a limit. It contains four learning targets:
    L1
    I can use a graph or table to determine the limit (one-sided or two-sided) of a function.
    L2
    I can use limit laws and algebraic techniques to compute the one-sided or two-sided limit of a function.
    L3
    I can determine when and if a function will have a vertical or horizontal asymptote.
    L4
    I can identify where a function is contininuous (on an interval or at a point), and describe the type of discontinuity.
